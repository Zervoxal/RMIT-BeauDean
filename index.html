Profile

Beau Dean - RMIT: S3858794
Beau.Dean@outlook.com.au
I'm just another fun, outgoing aussie IT lad trying to overcome IT stereotypes.

Beau is an active NSW RFS Fire Fighter
Support your local brigade today

With over six years of full-time employment history in IT, Beau has been in multiple sectors from Private Education, Enterprise Finance, and enabling businesses solutions within the Managed Service Providers workplace. Beau found his calling for IT as young as five years old when he blew up his first PC, trying to figure out what a voltage regulator switch on the back of a PSU does. Since then, Beau has been relentlessly pursuing further education in IT.

Beau has enrolled in his Bachelors of Information Technology with the Royal Melbourne Institute of Technology because he feels that the university and his values align together. Additionally, being a full-time employee, it is critically important to be flexible while managing university studies. He hopes to be able to achieve the absolute most out of his potential learning gain at RMIT while still implementing business IT solutions to his customers.
