Who is Beau Dean and how can he benefit your workplace or team?

Personality of Beau Dean

INTJ-T: Myers-Briggs Type Indicator is a self-report questionnaire which demonstrates our different psychological preferences.

Visual Learning Style - Beau prefers to approach learning through visual interaction. These can include graphs, data, images, etc.

Beau Dean - Everything DiSC WorkPlace Profile - 2020 Edition - DiSC is a globally leading and recognised tool used for identifiying individual communication styles and being able to tailor communication to specific individuals. Beau Dean is (D)riven Personality Style in DiSC.

What do these test results mean to me?

Beau Dean is a strong-willed, results driven individual with a high sense of accomplishment. Beau approaches any new challenge with an "under-promise, over-deliver" mind-set. While Beau Dean is a "go-getter" in some circles, it can also have it's downsides. Since Beau has such a strong sense of results delivery, other personality types that don't have the same drive and motivation may appear confrontational and appear to stand in the way of service delivery to Beau. It is critically important that Beau is to help motivate a team, without appearing to them as a dictator. When forming a team, Beau will tend to take lead and find personalities that do not conflict with his own.

Pro's of Beau

  • Highly Motivated and Results Driven.
  • Thrives in Fast-Paced Envirnoments.
  • Independent and Decisive.
  • Takes control when situation requires it.

Con's of Beau

  • Questioning and Skeptical.
  • Outspoken and Arrogant.
  • Loathes Highly Structured Envirnoments
  • Despises having to complete tasks repeatedly.
